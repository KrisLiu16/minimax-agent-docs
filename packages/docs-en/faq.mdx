## Product Scope

### What questions can the support bot answer?

It answers product usage, feature inquiries, credits & billing, and technical errors. It does **not** handle: image/video generation requests, casual chat, or topics unrelated to the product.

### What is the relationship between MaxClaw and OpenClaw?

MaxClaw is the cloud-hosted version of OpenClaw, powered by MiniMax's own models. For self-hosted local deployment, install OpenClaw yourself (see openclaw.ai).

### Are MiniMax Agent credits and MiniMax Open Platform balance interchangeable?

**No.** They are completely separate systems:

| | MiniMax Agent (MaxClaw) | MiniMax Open Platform |
|--|--|--|
| URL | agent.minimaxi.com | platform.minimaxi.com |
| Billing unit | Credits | RMB balance |
| Use case | Use MaxClaw | Call API / local deployment |

## Account & Login

### Are mobile app and web data synced?

Yes. The MaxClaw web app and the official MiniMax mobile app share the same account — data, credits, and conversation history are all synced.

### How do I get my chat_id for support?

Open a conversation and copy the number at the end of the URL.
Example: `https://agent.minimaxi.com/chat?id=370943808623550` → chat_id is `370943808623550`

⚠️ For any technical issue, support needs your chat_id before investigating further.
